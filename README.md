# 🎯 Where's Wally? Break the AI Challenge!

![Wally](images/finding_waldo/waldo.png)

**Can YOU outsmart an AI trained to find Wally?**

Welcome to the ultimate AI challenge! You'll be like a hacker, trying to trick a computer vision AI that thinks it's an expert at finding Wally. Your mission: make Wally invisible to the AI using shrinking, blurring, and other sneaky tricks!

## 🤔 What's This All About?

Imagine teaching a robot to spot Wally in "Where's Wally" pictures. It got pretty good... but can it handle YOUR tricks?

In this challenge, you'll:
- 🔬 **Shrink Wally** until the AI loses track of him
- 🌫️ **Blur him out** like a fuzzy photo
- 🎲 **Predict** how confident the AI will be
- 🏆 **Earn points** for your team and climb the leaderboard!

Think of it like a game where you're finding the AI's weaknesses. The smaller you can make Wally without breaking detection, the more points you score!

## 🚀 How to Get Started

### Step 1: Open VS Code
1. Find the **VS Code** icon on your computer and double-click it
2. You should see a window open with folders on the left side

### Step 2: Open the Competition Folder
1. Click **File** → **Open Folder** (top left corner)
2. Navigate to and select the **FindWaldo** folder
3. Click **Open**

### Step 3: Find the Competition Notebook
1. Look at the left sidebar in VS Code (called the "Explorer")
2. Find the file called **`CompetitionNotebook.ipynb`**
3. Double-click it to open!

### Step 4: Start the Challenge!
1. You'll see the notebook open with different sections
2. To run code, click inside a cell (the grey boxes)
3. Press **`Ctrl + Enter`** on your keyboard
4. Follow the instructions in each challenge!

**💡 Tip**: If you see a message asking to "Select Kernel", choose **Python 3** from the list!

## 🎮 The Challenges

### 🔍 Challenge 1: The Shrinking Game
**Mission**: Make Wally TINY but still detectable!

You get **3 guesses** to find the smallest Wally size the AI can still see. Think you can find the perfect size?

- Too big = AI finds him easily (boring! 😴)
- Too small = AI can't see him (you win! 🎉)
- Perfect size = Maximum points! (legendary! 🏆)

**What you'll learn**: Why size matters to AI vision!

---

### 🌫️ Challenge 2: The Blur Attack
**Mission**: Blur Wally just enough to confuse the AI!

Like when someone takes a blurry photo on their phone - how blurry before you can't see Wally anymore?

- Light blur = AI still spots him
- Medium blur = Getting tricky... 
- Perfect blur = AI is confused! You score big!

**What you'll learn**: How AI "sees" details (or doesn't!)

---

### 🎯 Challenge 3: Confidence Prediction
**Mission**: Predict how confident the AI will be!

Look at 4 different Wally images and guess: "How sure will the AI be that it found Wally?"

- 100% = AI is super confident
- 50% = AI is unsure
- 0% = AI has no idea!

**What you'll learn**: What makes some images "easy" vs "hard" for AI!

---

### 🎨 Challenge 4: Mystery Image Analysis
**Mission**: Figure out WHY the AI failed!

Look at a tricky image where the AI struggles. Can you spot what's confusing it?

Is it:
- Too many red and white stripes?
- Wally is too small?
- He's hiding behind something?
- The image is too blurry or messy?

**What you'll learn**: Think like an AI to understand its weaknesses!

## 🏆 Scoring & Leaderboard

Every challenge earns points for your team:
- **Perfect strategy** = Max points
- **Close attempts** = Fewer points
- **Brave tries** = Least points

Your team's total score will be displayed on the **Slido leaderboard**!
